What we do

PBC builds long-term value for intellectual property through strategy, partnerships, creative production, and a product-minded approach to execution.

IP strategy & licensing

We help global creators and studios enter Taiwan with clarity: positioning, partner selection, negotiation support, and licensing operations designed to protect the IP and unlock growth.

Brand collaborations

We design collaborations that feel natural to the IP and valuable to the brand — from concept development to partner alignment, deal structure, and launch planning.

Creative production

When execution matters, we support content and campaign production with a focus on consistency, speed, and quality — from social storytelling to activation assets.

Technology & systems

We work like a studio with startup DNA. That means clean workflows, clear documentation, lightweight tooling, and repeatable systems that make partnerships easier to scale.

How we work

We prefer small teams, tight timelines, and practical problem-solving. If the goal is growth, we focus on what moves the needle: trust, distribution, product-market fit, and sustainable operations.
